What's Inside

Everything You Need to Excel

Zenova packs 10 powerful tools into one clean app — built to help students focus, stay organised, and stay motivated.

⏱
Focus Timer
Powered by the Pomodoro technique, the focus timer helps you concentrate in structured sprints so you never burn out.
🃏
Flashcards
Create and review digital flashcards for any subject. Perfect for memorisation, revision, and exam prep.
📅
Exam Countdown
Set your upcoming exams and watch the countdown. Never be caught off-guard — always be prepared.
💬
Motivational Words
Start every study session with a fresh daily quote handpicked to keep your mindset strong and your energy high.
🎧
Audio Motivation
Listen to curated study and positivity audios. Let the right energy fuel your focus while you work.
🎯
Goal Tracker
Set weekly and monthly study goals, then watch your progress in real time. Big dreams, broken into daily actions.
📝
Notes Section
Capture ideas, summaries, and key points instantly. Your digital notebook is always just a tap away.
✅
To-Do Lists
Organise your daily study sessions with smart checklists. Tick tasks off and feel the satisfaction of progress.
🌿
Skincare & Daily Routine Tracker
Students are whole people. Track your daily self-care routines and keep every area of your life in order.
🏆
Gamification
Earn XP points and badges for completing tasks. Learning becomes addictive when there's a reward system behind it.
